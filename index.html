Prophet T.B Joshua



A look into Prophet T.B Joshua's life

  • June 12th, 1963 -Born in Ondo State, Nigeria

  • 1987- Prophet T. B. Joshua visited a prayer mountain to seek the face of God, where he fasted and prayed for 40 days and 40 nights.

  • March 8th, 2007 - Prophet T. B. Joshua founded a Christian television network, called Emmanuel TV. Emmanuel TV is the television station of SCOAN and broadcasts 24/7.

  • 2014 - A guesthouse collapsed within the SCOAN premises, resulting in the death of several people. T.B. Joshua and the church faced criticism and legal challenges in the aftermath.

  • Saturday 5th June 2021 - Prophet T. B. Joshua spoke during the Emmanuel TV Partners Meeting: “Time for everything – time to come here for prayer and time to return home after the service”. God took His servant Prophet T. B. Joshua home – as it should be by divine will
